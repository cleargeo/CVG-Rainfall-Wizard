⚙️ How It Works

Three steps from coordinates to design storm — no PFDS lookups, no GIS interpolation, no spreadsheets.

1
Set Your Location
Provide a lat/lon point or bounding box AOI. The engine automatically identifies the applicable NOAA Atlas 14 region and nearest PFDS grid nodes.
2
Select Design Criteria
Choose return period(s), storm duration(s), and hyetograph distribution (Huff / SCS Type). Optionally request a spatial raster output for watershed-wide analysis.
3
Retrieve & Model
Download depth tables (CSV/JSON), gridded rasters (GeoTIFF), or full hyetographs (JSON). Import directly into HEC-HMS, SWMM, ICPR, or any standard hydrology platform.
